Understanding Tariffs

What are Tariffs?

Tariffs are taxes imposed by governments on imported goods. They're designed to protect domestic industries by making imported products more expensive.

Current Trade Tensions

Recent years have seen escalating trade tensions between major economies, with tariffs being used as economic and political tools.

Business Impact

Tariffs can significantly impact supply chains, pricing strategies, and market competitiveness for businesses engaged in international trade.
